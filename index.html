The system captured customer registrations and added them into follow-up email flows, allowing consistent engagement through newsletters, promotions, and post-purchase communication. Results: Increased the filter sale by 5%. Gained direct visibility into end-customer relationships. Enabled targeted upselling and customer support initiatives.

Customer Experience Program

A structured post-purchase email journey was created to strengthen customer engagement and retention. The process included capturing customer data from multiple CRMs into a centralized Google Sheet that synced directly with Mailchimp. Different email flows were built for customers inside and outside the service area, along with timed messages at 14 days, 6 months, and 12 months after delivery. Automation was set up to send the right emails at the right time without manual work, supporting customer check-ins, feedback collection, and ongoing communication.

Results: Achieved a 12% increase in CTR and a 9% boost in conversion rate within one month.
